Our history

Hello. My name is Dana, I am a mother of 3 children.

Having not found suitable, natural, non-toxic toys for our children in our market, we decided to make them ourselves! What a surprise and delight it was when our children simply did not let these toys out of their hands!

Meet Nursulu 👋🏻 - my sister and partner, Mother of the beautiful princess Ayala. So, together with Nursulu, the idea was born: to give the opportunity for tactile experience to other children, while being sure that it is 100% high-quality wood, only natural, natural and non-toxic materials.

Payment and delivery

How we are working?

For consultation and order connect with us: WhatsApp или Instagram. After payment, our manager will confirm your order, and you can choose the delivery time.

What about the delivery?

For Astana (Nur-Sultan) delivery is carried out by the Yandex.Courier service. If you are from another city, the shipment will be carried out by a courier service (1500-2000 tenge depending on your city). Delivery is paid separately.

How long does it take to ship the goods?

Every Tuesday and Thursday we ship orders placed that week.

How to get a discount?

We provide our customers with a cashback of 5% of the cost of goods for a review in their stories indicating our account: @woody_alen_toys
